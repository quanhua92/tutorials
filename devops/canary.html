① Weighted router — step through a canary rollout

What to do: pick a scenario and drag the stage slider. The router splits traffic by weight: v1 stable gets (100−w)%, v2 canary gets w%. HEALTHY ramps all the way to 100%. UNHEALTHY aborts at 25% and rolls back to 0 — watch the canary arrows shrink back.

② The analysis — error rate & latency vs the v1 baseline, per stage

What this shows: at each analysed stage, v2's error rate (left) and p95 latency (right) are compared to the v1 baseline (dashed) and the abort threshold (2× err / 1.5× lat). Bars under the threshold and with enough weight (≥5%) PROMOTE; over it ABORT. In the unhealthy run the 25% bar blows past both thresholds.
v1 baseline: err 0.5%, p95 100ms · abort if err > 1.0% or lat > 150ms (and weight ≥ 5%)

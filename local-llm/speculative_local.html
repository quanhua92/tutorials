② Why it works: decode is memory-bound (batch γ+1 ≈ batch 1)

A decode pass streams the weight matrix from RAM either way. The activation for a few extra tokens is negligible. So verifying γ+1 tokens costs ~the same as generating 1 — the weight read is amortised across the whole batch. This is the entire premise of speculative decoding.

③ Speedup analysis — effective vs net (drag the draft size)

Effective (naive) = tokens/step if the draft were free = 1 + Σpk. Net (real) divides by the draft's own γ passes. Drag the draft size to see the trade-off.

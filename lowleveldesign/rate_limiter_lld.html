2. Strategy Comparison — one stream, three policies

The same 8-request stream (three rapid, then spaced out) through every policy. Mirrors rate_limiter_lld_output.txt (Section “Strategy comparison”). Token bucket is most permissive under spaced load because it refills continuously; the window policies deny until the window resets/slides.

Reading: a boundary burst (Section 3 of the guide) is where fixed and sliding diverge — fixed lets a fresh window's full quota through instantly; sliding makes the oldest request age out first. Token bucket neither bursts nor starves: it smooths to the sustained rate.

Informed search: f(n) = g(n) + h(n). A* expands fewer cells than Dijkstra by steering toward the goal with a heuristic, yet stays optimal when h never overestimates. Toggle the view to watch the wall force a detour, and to see exactly which cells Dijkstra (h=0) wastes.  |  ← Dijkstra (h=0)

① The 7×9 grid — A* threads a corridor through the wall

What to do: pick a view. blue = expanded (with its g), green = the optimal path (cost 18), orange = cells only Dijkstra bothered with. Drag the heuristic slider to see inadmissible (overestimating) "Weighted A*" prune harder.

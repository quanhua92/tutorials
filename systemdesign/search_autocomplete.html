① Live typeahead — type a prefix, walk the trie, get suggestions

What to do: type into the box (try ap, wo, g, z). The trie walks one hop per character and reads the cached top-K at the landing node — that is the whole O(L) lookup. Suggestions re-rank under whichever ranking layer is selected in panel ②. A shard badge shows where the request routes.

③ Prefix sharding — split the trie by first character

Click a shard to see its terms and frequency load. One trie cannot serve global QPS, so the trie is split by the first character into 4 range shards; apple→shard0, google→shard1. Range sharding routes in O(1) but is skew-prone — load follows letter popularity, so a-f runs hot while n-s idles.

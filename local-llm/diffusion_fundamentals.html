② Step count vs quality — the speed/quality tradeoff

What this shows: relative FID quality (100 steps = 100% reference) for a single model under different step counts. 30 steps already reaches 97% — the sweet spot. The 4-step tier needs a distilled (Lightning/Turbo) checkpoint.
Curve is concave (diminishing returns): going 30→100 steps adds only 3% quality but ~3× the time. DDPM's 1000 steps sit far off the right edge of this chart — that's why schedulers exist.

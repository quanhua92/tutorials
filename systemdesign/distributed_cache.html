① Consistent Hashing Ring — add / remove nodes, watch keys redistribute

What to do: click + Add node or − Remove node. Key dots that change owner flash gold. Consistent hashing moves only ~K/N keys; hash modulo N would move ~3× more.

② LRU Cache — HashMap + doubly-linked list, O(1) get/put

What to do: step through. Boxes are ordered MRU → LRU. When the cache is full (cap=3), the rightmost box (LRU) is evicted. A HIT promotes the key to MRU; a MISS inserts at MRU and may evict from LRU.

③ Hit Rate → Latency Simulator

Drag the slider. Cache hit ≈ 0.25 ms, cache miss ≈ 0.25 + 5.0 = 5.25 ms (cache check + DB fetch). Effective latency = hit_rate × 0.25 + miss_rate × 5.25.

④ Cache Stampede — naive vs probabilistic early expiration

200 readers arrive as a hot key's TTL expires. Naive: all readers within the 5s fetch window miss and pile onto the DB (thundering herd). PEE: readers in [soft, TTL] refresh with P = (t−soft)/(TTL−soft), warming the cache before the herd arrives.

② Height meter — BST goes linear, RB stays logarithmic

Bars are normalized to the bound 2·log₂(N+1). The red BST bar hits the ceiling under sorted input; the green RB bar never crosses the dashed bound line (CLRS Theorem 13.1).

③ What the RB tree did on the last insert (the fixup trace)

CLRS RB-INSERT-FIXUP picks a case by looking at the uncle: red uncle → recolor (Case 1); black uncle, inner child → rotate parent (Case 2 → Case 3); black uncle, outer child → recolor + rotate grandparent (Case 3). At most one rotation per insert.

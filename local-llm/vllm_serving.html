② The two innovations that make the curve grow

PagedAttention pages the KV cache in fixed 16-token blocks, killing the 60–80% waste of contiguous worst-case reservation → far more concurrent requests fit on the same GPU (bigger batches). Continuous batching admits a queued request into a freed slot the very next iteration → no head-of-line blocking, the batch stays full. Together they let the batch amortise one weight read across many sequences.

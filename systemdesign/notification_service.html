② Priority queue — urgent leap-frogs the FIFO backlog

③ Preferences + dedup — the matrix filter and the idempotency key

Toggle U3's email-marketing preference and watch n4 flip between suppressed (0 channels) and delivered. Toggle dedup and the duplicate n1 either collapses (1 idempotency key) or double-fans-out. Preferences are a channel × category matrix, not a single on/off — that's why n4 can be suppressed while U3 still gets mention emails.

③ Politeness rate visualizer — per-domain crawl-delay spacing

What to do: set the per-domain crawl_delay with the sliders. The timeline shows when each domain's URLs get fetched. Observation: at t=0, all three domains fetch simultaneously (parallel). Then each domain's subsequent fetches are spaced by its own delay. A domain with delay=5s fetches every 5s; delay=1s fetches every 1s. They don't block each other.

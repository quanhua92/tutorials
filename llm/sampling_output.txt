========================================================================
  SECTION E: top-p=0.6 nucleus  (keep smallest set, cumulative prob >= p)
========================================================================
Algorithm (matches tiny-llm make_sampler):
  1. sort tokens DESCENDING by probability
  2. compute cumulative sum of the PROBABILITIES (NOT logprobs!)
  3. keep tokens where cumulative prob < p
  4. ALWAYS keep the top-1 (nucleus is never empty)

Top-p = 0.6

========================================================================
  SECTION F: PITFALL — cumsum on LOGPROBS (wrong) vs PROBS (right)
========================================================================
The single most common top-p bug: accumulate the LOG-probabilities
instead of the probabilities. Log-probs are NEGATIVE (every logprob
< 0), so their cumulative sum stays negative forever and is ALWAYS
less than any positive p. Result: NOTHING gets masked — the 'nucleus'
is the entire vocabulary. Silent, no error.

p = 0.6

|------|-----|-------|---------|---------------|-----|----------|------------------|-----|

| 1    | 0   | the   | 0.2377  |        0.5281 | yes | -1.4367  |          -2.6734 | yes |
| 2    | 1   | cat   | 0.1761  |        0.7042 | no  | -1.7367  |          -4.4100 | yes |

========================================================================
  SECTION G: combined pipeline + seeded sample (the only RNG step)
========================================================================
Production order (tiny-llm make_sampler):
  1. temp==0  -> return argmax (greedy, no RNG)
  2. top-k    -> mask logits to -inf outside top-k
  3. top-p    -> mask logits to -inf outside the nucleus
  4. divide remaining logprobs by temp
  5. categorical draw (THE only random step)

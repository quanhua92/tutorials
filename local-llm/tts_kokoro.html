① The pipeline — type text, watch every stage

What this shows: the full text→speech funnel, stage by stage, with the data shape change at each step (text → symbols → frames → matrix → waveform). Same front-end stages as tts_kokoro.py Section A: normalization (misaki), G2P phonemization, duration prediction, acoustic model, ISTFTNet vocoder.
Stage 1 expands symbols to words ($5→five dollars). Stage 2 is G2P (grapheme→phoneme). Stage 3 is the duration predictor (frames per phoneme). Stage 4 is the acoustic model (StyleTTS 2 decoder). Stage 5 is the ISTFTNet vocoder.

② Prosody — phoneme durations (drag to reshape)

What this shows: the StyleTTS 2 duration predictor's output — how many mel frames each phoneme spans. Each slider is one phoneme; the total drives the mel width and the waveform length. Defaults match DURATION_BY_PHONEME in tts_kokoro.py.
Consonants (e.g. /h/) are short; vowels and diphthongs (e.g. /ʊ/, /aɪ/) are long. The /oʊ/ diphthong is split into onset /o/ + glide /ʊ/ so the predictor can model the two phases separately.

③ Mel-spectrogram — 80-band frequency grid (the acoustic model output)

What this shows: the mel-spectrogram the acoustic model produces — frames (columns) × 80 mel bands (rows), color = energy. Each phoneme contributes a horizontal stripe at its formant band(s); the duration predictor sets the stripe width. This is the classic TTS spectrogram look.
low freq (band 0) mel band (rows) — high freq (band 79)
0 energy low mid high orange ramp = energy
One mel frame = 256 samples = 10.67 ms. The colormap is an orange ramp (Kokoro accent); brighter = more energy at that frequency.

④ Vocoder — mel-spectrogram → waveform (ISTFTNet)

What this shows: the ISTFTNet vocoder inverts the mel-spectrogram into a 1-D waveform at 24 000 samples/sec. One mel frame expands to 256 consecutive samples. The three cards below recompute the sample/duration math live as you drag the duration sliders.
Waveform preview: amplitude = per-frame mel energy, oscillating at a base F0. Real Kokoro uses inverse-STFT; this is a faithful shape preview for teaching.

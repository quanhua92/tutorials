② Booking conflict simulator — the last-room thundering herd, 3 strategies

What to do: drag the slider to set how many users hit the last remaining room on the same night, then hit resolve. Pessimistic locks serialize and exhaust the pool; Optimistic CAS retries (~40K DB ops); Redis DECRBY gates the DB to exactly 1 op (hotel_booking.py Section 4).
Press resolve to process the last-room spike three ways.

③ Overbooking visualizer — max_bookable + no-show z-score alert

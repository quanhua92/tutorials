Wire up every vertex for the least total weight. Kruskal sorts edges and skips cycle-closers (Union-Find); Prim grows a blob from the source via a priority queue. Both O(E log V), both optimal via the cut property. On a tie-graph they can pick different edges of the same weight.  |  ← Bellman-Ford

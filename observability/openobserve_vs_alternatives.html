What this shows: monthly TCO (storage + compute + license) as you scale ingest volume + retention. S3-native engines keep compute ~flat (scales with ingest rate); ES/Splunk compute grows with stored data; SaaS is priced per GB ingested. Identical model to openobserve_vs_alternatives.py Section D.

② Dedup — SimHash near-duplicate comparison (GOLD)

What this proves: SimHash hashes each token and takes a per-bit majority vote → an order-invariant 64-bit fingerprint. Two articles with a small hamming distance (≤ 3) are the same story, even if a few words were rewritten. ④ Adaptive crawl scheduling — poll interval ∝ update rate

Drag the update rate for one feed. The poll interval is set to ~2× the update rate (clamped to [15, 1440] min), so a fast wire (48/day) is polled every 15 min but a daily blog only once a day. Combined with conditional GET, this cuts bandwidth ~81% vs naive "poll everything every 15 min".

③ Distributed — why naive GET-then-INCR races (and Lua fixes it)

Scenario: 100 concurrent requests against a limit of 50. With a naive GET-then-INCR, all 100 read the counter before any write lands → all 100 pass (100% overshoot). With an atomic Lua check-and-incr, exactly 50 pass. Toggle and watch.

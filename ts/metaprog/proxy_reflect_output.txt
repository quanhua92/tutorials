proxy_reflect.ts — Phase 6 bundle (metaprog member).
Every intercept below is computed by this file; the .md guide pastes
it verbatim. Nothing is hand-computed.

Proxy traps the FUNDAMENTAL operations on an object (get/set/has/delete/
ownKeys/apply/construct); Reflect forwards a trap back to the default.
No Math.random / Date.now — output is deterministic.

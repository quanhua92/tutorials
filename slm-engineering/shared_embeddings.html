① Tie / untie the vocabulary matrices — one object or two?

How to use: toggle tied / untied. Untied keeps TWO independent [V, H] matrices (embed + lm_head) = 2·V·H params. Tied rebinds lm_head.weight = embed.weight so ONE matrix wears both hats = V·H params (and embed.weight is lm_head.weight → True, the structural check). Drag V & H to watch the live param counter.

How to read: each row is a real model's vocab block. ties? comes straight from each model's config.json (tie_word_embeddings). SmolLM2-1.7B, Gemma-2-2B, GPT-2 tie; Llama-3-8B does NOT — at 8B scale the budget permits two matrices with cleaner per-path gradients. Savings = V·H, recomputed live.

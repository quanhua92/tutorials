Belady's anomaly — more frames can mean MORE faults

Using the Belady reference string with FIFO: going from 3 to 4 frames increases faults. LRU and Clock are stack algorithms — more frames always helps (or stays equal).

Page table — 4 KB pages, virtual addr = 4-bit VPN | 12-bit offset

Invalid VPNs (not in table) trigger a page fault. The low 12-bit offset is carried unchanged into the physical address.

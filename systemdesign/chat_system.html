② Delivery status — sent → delivered → read (forward only)

What to do: click each step to advance a message's status. The state machine only moves forward; try the illegal read → delivered transition — it is rejected (chat_system.py Section 3).

What to do: drag the group-size slider. Write cost scales linearly with members for fan-out-on-write; a 1000-member group burns 1000× the writes of a 1:1 chat (chat_system.py Section 6).

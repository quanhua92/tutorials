① The sort, frame by frame

What to do: press play or drag the scrubber. Each frame is one comparison of an adjacent pair (orange). If the pair is out of order it swaps (red). A green cap marks bars already locked in their final position at the tail.
unsorted bar comparing pair swapping pair locked at tail
Invariant: after pass k, the last k+1 bars are the largest, in final order. If a whole pass swaps nothing, the early-exit flag stops the sort (best case O(n)).

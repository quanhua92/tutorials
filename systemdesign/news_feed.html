③ The celebrity problem — push writes explode, hybrid stays at 1

Drag the follower count. Under push, a single post = one feed-list write PER follower — it crosses 1,000,000 writes for a celebrity. Under hybrid, once followers cross the threshold (1M), the author flips to pull and the write cost collapses to 1. This is why hyperscale feeds are hybrid.

## A. Move 1 ‚ÄĒ PROBLEM (framing the challenge)

evidence**, not indirectly. Confirmed across Purdue OWL ("A proposal should
state the problem and its significance") and the Oxford example-sentence corpus
(`The firm is struggling to cope with the demand`; `We need to adopt a new

## D. Move 4 ‚ÄĒ ASK / NEXT STEPS (the close)

entry (*"The plan will be submitted to the committee for official approval"*;

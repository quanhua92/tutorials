The apprentice idea: a small SLM (draft) guesses γ tokens cheaply, then the big target verifies all γ in ONE parallel pass. We keep the agreed prefix and resample the first mismatch. The gold pin: γ=4 → accept R=3 → 4 tokens per target forward. Every number is recomputed in JS from the identical formula in speculative_draft.py.  |  compare with MLX/Metal edge runtime →

② Speedup: drag γ + α + κ → live tokens/forward and speedup S

How to read: E[tokens/forward] = (1−α^(γ+1))/(1−α) (the geometric series; +1 is the bonus/resample token). S = E[tokens/forward] / (1 + γ/κ) over plain 1-token/step decode. The GOLD pin: the toy round commits 4 tokens in 1 target pass (R=3).

③ Speedup grid S(α, γ) at κ=8 — where spec wins

How to read: with κ=8 (draft 8× cheaper) and γ=4, the round costs 1 + 4/8 = 1.5 target-pass-equivalents — spec wins once E[tokens] > 1.5 (α ≳ 0.4). At α=0.9, γ=4 gives S=2.73 (matches speculative_draft.py Section D).

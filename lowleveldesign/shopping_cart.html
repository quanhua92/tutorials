Shopping Cart & Checkout — Simulator + State Machine

Add items from the catalog, apply coupon codes, and watch the pricing rules engine compute the breakdown live (BOGO, bulk discount, percent-off, fixed-off, free-shipping threshold). Below, drive the cart state machine through every legal transition. The cart is a mutable scratch pad; the order it produces is an immutable notarized receipt — never conflate the two.

1. Cart Simulator — pricing rules engine, live

Click a product to add it. Use +/- to change quantity. Apply coupon codes SAVE15 (15% off) and WELCOME5 ($5 off). The engine runs LINE rules first (BOGO, bulk), then CART rules on the post-line subtotal — exactly as in shopping_cart.py.

2. Cart State Machine

The five cart states and the legal transitions between them. The amber node is the current state; click a transition button to drive it. Illegal transitions (e.g. EMPTY → PAID) are disabled. This mirrors STATE_TRANSITIONS in shopping_cart.py.

3. Pricing Rules — the Strategy family

Each rule is a PromotionRule strategy plugged into the PromotionEngine. The Cart knows nothing about these mechanics (SRP). Add a new promotion by writing one class — no edits to Cart or Engine (OCP).

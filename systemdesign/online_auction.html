② Concurrent bid conflict — same 3 bids, 3 resolution strategies

What to do: hit resolve. Three bids land concurrently on an auction at $100 (Alice); network lag makes the lowest bid ($110 Carol) arrive last. Last-write-wins lets her overwrite Bob's $130 — the seller loses $20. Kafka partition processes sequentially and rejects bids that don't clear the running price (online_auction.py Section 3).

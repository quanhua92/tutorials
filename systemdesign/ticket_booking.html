Pick seats on the seat map (hold them with a TTL, watch the sweeper release them, pay to confirm), replay the concurrent-booking race three ways, walk the payment state machine, and click through the architecture — every number recomputes in JS from the identical logic as ticket_booking.py, gold-checked below.

① Seat map — select, hold (TTL), pay to confirm

What to do: click available seats to select them, hit hold selected. They turn emerald (HELD) with a TTL countdown. Hit pay & confirm to convert HELD→SOLD, or let the timer hit 0 to watch the sweeper release them. ② Concurrent booking race — same seat, 3 resolution strategies

What to do: hit resolve. Alice and Bob race to hold seat S1 (AVAILABLE, v0). Naive read-modify-write lets Bob's stale read overwrite Alice's hold — both think they own it (double-book). SELECT FOR UPDATE is correct but Bob blocks. Redis Lua CAS runs check-and-set atomically — Alice wins, Bob rejected instantly, no lock wait (ticket_booking.py Section 3).
base: S1 = AVAILABLE, v0  |  arrivals: Alice (1), Bob (2)
Press resolve to process the race three ways.

③ Payment state machine — authorize on hold, capture on confirm, void on expiry

What to do: click a scenario. Happy path authorizes then captures (funds move). Hold expiry voids the authorization (user never charged). Decline fails at PENDING. Refund reverses a capture. Illegal transitions are blocked by guards (ticket_booking.py Section 5).

③ Step 2 — Embed (fake hashing vectors)

A real embedder (nomic-embed-text, 768 dims) needs PyTorch. Here we use the feature-hashing trick: each lowercase word → FNV-1a hash → one of 256 dims → count → L2-normalize. Two texts sharing words land in the same buckets, so cosine tracks lexical overlap. Deterministic (FNV-1a is NOT Python's randomized hash).

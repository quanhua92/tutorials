① The sort, frame by frame

What to do: press play or drag the scrubber. Each frame is one comparison of an adjacent pair (orange). If the left neighbour is larger, they swap (red) and the card keeps walking left; the moment it finds a smaller (or equal) neighbour, it stops.
sorted / working prefix comparing pair swapping pair untouched tail
Invariant: after step i, arr[0..i] is sorted (blue). Unlike bubble sort, no element is permanently locked until the very end — the next card may still shift it. Best case (already sorted) is O(n): one comparison per card.

📖 Prefer to read first? Open the full written guide — intuition, math, derivations, and references — then return here to experiment interactively.
ARC runs an LRU half (T1) and an LFU half (T2) at once, keeps ghosts (B1/B2) of every eviction, and retargets an adaptive dial p from ghost re-hits. Step the adaptation workload and watch p climb on Case-II (B1) hits and drop on Case-III (B2) hits. Re-run live in JS, identical to arc_cache.py.  |  ← LFU's pollution bug, the thing ARC fixes

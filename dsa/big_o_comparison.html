② Time-to-run at N=1000 — 1 op = 1 ns

What this proves: a 1 GHz core doing 1 op/ns makes every polynomial class trivial at N=1000 (under a millisecond), while the two exponential classes outlast the universe. This is the tractability cliff, recomputed live.

③ Master Theorem calculator — T(N) = a·T(N/b) + Θ(Nᶜ)

What to do: dial a (subproblems), b (shrink factor), c (combine-cost exponent). The page computes p = log_b(a), classifies the case, and prints the closed-form T(N). Try the presets.

④ Practical crossover — O(N²) insertion vs O(N log N) merge

① North Star Metric — WAEL and the 30-minute engagement threshold

What to do: drag the threshold slider. Users at/above it count as engaged (pink bars); below it are merely active (blue). Defaults reproduce north_star_metrics.py Section 1 (WAU 32,000 / WAEL 18,400 / engagement 57.5%).

② Metric tree builder — NSM → L1 drivers → L2 inputs

What to do: drag WAU, engagement, new-user share, activation. The tree re-renders: WAEL = WAU × engagement, split into new vs returning engaged. Defaults reproduce north_star_metrics.py Section 2 exactly (18,400 = 2,480 new + 15,920 returning).

③ Driver hierarchy — who owns what, how often (L0 / L1 / L2 / guardrail)

The four-layer measurement stack. Cadence matches decision speed: guardrails page on-call in minutes; the NSM rolls up weekly (north_star_metrics.py Section 6). Click a row to highlight its layer.

④ Guardrail dashboard — the floor that must NOT slip

What to do: drag any guardrail's current value past its gold target tick. Green = safe, orange = near, red = BREACH (feature does NOT ship). Defaults reproduce north_star_metrics.py Section 3 (skip 8.94%, p99 640ms, error 0.21%, churn 2.1%).

⑤ Leading vs lagging — the time-horizon map

Whisper STT — speech-to-text pipeline 📖 WHISPER_STT.md 📄 whisper_stt.py

An encoder-decoder Transformer turns audio into text: 16 kHz mono → log-mel spectrogram (80 × 3000 for 30 s) → encoder → autoregressive decoder (special tokens select transcribe/translate) → text. faster-whisper runs the same weights ~4× faster at ~50% VRAM (int8); on an RTX 4090 large-v3 hits RTF 0.03 = 33× real-time. Pick a model, drag the RTF, watch a clip transcribe. All values recomputed live in JS — identical to whisper_stt.py.

6L1 pitfalls & cheat sheet

Vietnamese → English pitfalls

Vietnamese trap (what you do)English fix (what to do instead)
Merges three tenses into one — "I'm doing the login" for finished, in-progress, AND forecast workSplit explicitly: I've finished / I'm currently working on / I'm on track for. One state per chunk.
Omits the ETA slot entirely — assumes the team shares the timeline (high-context norm)Always close with I should have it done by [date]. Name a concrete date; the modal should calibrates it as a forecast, not a promise.
Hides blockers to save face — won't say "I'm blocked" (feels like admitting incompetence)Reframe: in English-speaking agile, flagging a blocker on day one is the senior move. Use I'm blocked on… + what you need + who can unblock you.
Over-promises to please — "I will finish by tomorrow" with no real planCalibrate with the modal: I should have it done by… (forecast), not I will finish by… (promise). Miss a promise and you lose trust.
Vague verb "doing" — "I'm doing the task" with no state, no scope, no ETAReplace doing with a state-specific chunk: finished / currently working on / on track for.
Rambling 3-minute paragraph (the Vietnamese báo cáo style) instead of a 30-second structured turnForce the four-slot skeleton: FRAME → PROGRESS → BLOCKER → ETA. Past 60 seconds, you've skipped or merged a slot.
Confuses by and until — "I will finish until Friday" (Vietnamese đến conflates both)By Friday = deadline is Friday. Until Friday = continues for the whole duration up to Friday. ETA slots use by.
Drops the final cluster in blocked / finished — "I'm blok on…" / "I've finish on…"Drill the /kt/ in blocked /blɒkt/ and the /ʃt/ in finished /ˈfɪnɪʃt/. 🔗 Final Consonants.
No noun/verb stress shift on progress — says proGRESS for the nounThe noun is PROgress /ˈprəʊɡres/ (UK) · /ˈprɑːɡres/ (US) — stress on the first syllable. The verb flips it.
Translates the opener literally — "I report a little" / "Today I want to say…"Use a native framing chunk: Quick update on my side / Just a quick one / Status update.

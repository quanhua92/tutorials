② Negative cycle = currency arbitrage — the −log(rate) reduction

What this proves: a profitable trade loop is a negative cycle. w = −ln(rate), so Σw = −ln(Πrate) < 0 ⟺ Πrate > 1 ⟺ profit. The cycle USD→EUR→GBP→USD multiplies to 1.0653 — Bellman-Ford catches it on the V-th pass.

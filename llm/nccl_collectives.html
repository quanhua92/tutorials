The bucket brigade: K GPUs in a ring, each talking only to its neighbors, sum everyone's data onto everyone for ~2N bytes/GPU regardless of K. Drag K to see the 5 primitives; scrub the ring step slider to watch the sum propagate. Every number is recomputed in JS from the identical algorithm in nccl_collectives.py.  |  consumed by DDP (AllReduce) · Tensor Parallel (AllReduce/layer) · ZeRO (ReduceScatter+AllGather).

① The 5 primitives — before/after grids (drag K; distinct data per rank)

rank r holds [10r+1 … 10r+K]; N = K (chunks of 1). Matches nccl_collectives.py Section B.
What to do: flip through the 5 primitives. Broadcast overwrites everyone with rank 0. Reduce lands the sum only on the root (others dimmed = untouched). AllReduce = sum on everyone (DDP's only collective). ReduceScatter leaves each rank holding just its summed shard; AllGather rebuilds the full vector — together they equal AllReduce (panel ③).

② Ring-AllReduce — the GOLD example (K=4, N=16). Scrub the step slider.

K=4 ranks, all hold v=[1..16], chunk=4. After all 6 steps every rank = [4,8,…,64]. Matches nccl_collectives.py Section D.
Phases: steps 0–2 = scatter-reduce (each receiver +=s, accumulates); steps 3–5 = allgather (each receiver overwrites). At step 2 each rank fully owns one summed chunk; at step 5 every rank has the full sum.
What to watch: the chunk each rank sends RIGHT is highlighted; it lands on the next rank and is accumulated (phase 1) or copied (phase 2). The bytes/GPU counter ticks up by N/K=4 every step → 6 steps × 4 = 24 at the end (the gold).

③ Per-GPU bytes → ~2N (drag K), and the AllReduce = ReduceScatter + AllGather identity

Left table: per-GPU bytes = 2·(K−1)/K·N. As K grows the ratio to 2N → 1.0 — adding GPUs never balloons any GPU's traffic. Right readout: the identity — AllReduce output byte-identical to ReduceScatter→AllGather (NCCL_COLLECTIVES.md §4).

① Chunking strategy visualizer — fixed-size vs sentence-aware vs semantic

What to do: pick a strategy. The same 6-sentence demo doc (topic shift after sentence 3: embeddings → vector-db systems) is chunked three ways. Fixed-size slides a token window and splits mid-sentence; sentence-aware snaps to boundaries; semantic finds the topic boundary and groups sentences by meaning (rag_system.py Section 1).

② Retrieval pipeline — query → embed → cosine top-5 → cross-encoder rerank

What to do: the query "vector retrieval similarity search" is embedded (TF-IDF), every chunk scored by cosine similarity, top-5 taken, then a cross-encoder re-scores them by idf-weighted query coverage. Watch c3 get promoted above c10: cosine ranked the terse c10 higher, but coverage (one query term each) breaks the tie by index — the cross-encoder lifts chunks that the bi-encoder ranked low (rag_system.py Sections 3–4).

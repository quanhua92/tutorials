Killer gotchas (verbatim from design.py Section D):
  1. Delete empty frequency buckets in LFU, or min_freq can point at a bucket with no victim.
  2. On a bump, only raise min_freq if the emptied bucket was at min_freq.
  3. A new key always resets min_freq = 1.
  4. Update ≠ insert: put on an existing key refreshes/bumps but never grows size or evicts.
  5. Handle cap ≤ 0 at the top of put — LeetCode tests capacity 0.
  6. DLL sentinels (dummy head/tail) remove every empty-list edge case.
  7. LFU tie-break needs recency within a frequency — that's why each bucket is an OrderedDict / DLL.

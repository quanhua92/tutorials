Log-Structured Merge-tree (LSM) — interactive LSM_TREE.md lsm_tree.py

Writes append to a MemTable → flush to immutable L0 SSTables (overlapping) → background compaction merge-sorts them into disjoint levels (each ~T× bigger). Reads walk MemTable → L0 (every file) → L1+ (one by range), stopping at the first hit — a value or a tombstone. The page rebuilds the exact canonical workload from lsm_tree.py in JS and gold-checks every query.

📖 Read the full guide — architecture diagrams, decision tables, killer gotchas, cheat sheet. This page is the interactive companion.
A B+tree keeps data only in the leaves and chains the leaves into one sorted list (the "+"). Internal nodes are just dividers. Slide order m (3–6), step the inserts one key at a time and watch splits propagate up, then run a search to see the root→leaf path light up, or a range scan to watch the leaf chain walk. The tree is rebuilt live in JS with the identical algorithm and insert order as btree.py Section A.  |  what an index points at →

Last structural event: —. A leaf overflow copies the separator up; an internal overflow moves it up; a root split makes a new root and the tree grows taller by one level.
